About Steering & Suspension at Bingo Tire & Auto Service

Expert steering and suspension repair at all five Bingo Tire & Auto Service locations in Northern Virginia. We repair and replace shocks, struts, tie rod ends, ball joints, control arm bushings, sway bar links, and power steering components on all makes and models. Drivers in Springfield, Woodbridge, Alexandria, Centreville, and Winchester trust Bingo Tire for safe, reliable suspension service.

Your vehicle's steering and suspension systems are responsible for keeping you in control on the road — especially on Northern Virginia's mix of smooth highways and rough city streets. Worn shocks and struts allow excessive body motion during cornering and braking, reducing your ability to control the vehicle in an emergency. Worn tie rod ends and ball joints introduce looseness into the steering that makes precise control difficult. At Bingo Tire & Auto Service, our technicians perform a thorough steering and suspension inspection to identify every worn or damaged component before it becomes a safety issue.

Suspension and steering repairs vary widely in complexity. Replacing a sway bar link is a simple, affordable job that can transform a clunking, uncomfortable ride. Replacing struts on a front-wheel drive vehicle requires compressing the spring — a job that must be done with professional equipment for safety reasons. Our technicians have the training, tools, and experience to handle every level of steering and suspension service from simple linkage replacements to full strut assembly replacements, always followed by a wheel alignment check to protect your tires.

Signs You May Need This Service

  • 1Rough, bumpy, or excessively bouncy ride quality
  • 2Vehicle nose dives when braking
  • 3Body rolls excessively when cornering
  • 4Clunking, knocking, or squeaking sounds over bumps
  • 5Uneven or accelerated tire wear patterns
  • 6Steering feels loose, vague, or wanders at highway speed
